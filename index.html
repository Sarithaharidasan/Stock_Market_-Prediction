This question has been in investors' minds for as long as
financial markets have existed. Who wouldn’t want consistently
to be able to buy low and sell high? A tool that enabled
an investor to make accurate predictions about market
fluctuations would be invaluable in building wealth and
financial freedom, for investors of any scale.Conventional wisdom
tells us that this dream can never be a reality. Almost every book
about personal investment will tell you that attempting to time the
stock market is a bad idea; that those who bet on predicting mass market
movement will lose money. Even sophisticated market analysis tools and
models are held to have no more accuracy than the toss of a coin.
